
        Important educational boards in India:
        
        1. CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education): National level board, follows NCERT curriculum
        2. ICSE/ISC (Indian Certificate of Secondary Education): Known for comprehensive English and Science education
        3. State Boards: Each state has its own education board with specific curriculum
        4. International Boards: IB (International Baccalaureate) and Cambridge (IGCSE)
        
        Key entrance exams after 10th include:
        - JEE Main & Advanced for engineering
        - NEET for medical
        - CLAT for law
        - NDA for defense
        - Various state-level entrance tests
