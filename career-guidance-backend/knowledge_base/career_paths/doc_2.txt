
        Commerce Career Paths in India:
        
        1. Chartered Accountancy:
           - B.Com → CA Foundation → CA Intermediate → CA Final → Assistant Manager → Manager → Partner
           - Entry: CA exams through ICAI
           - Skills: Accounting, taxation, auditing, finance
        
        2. Corporate Finance:
           - B.Com/BBA → MBA (Finance) → Financial Analyst → Finance Manager → CFO
           - Entry: B.Com, MBA
           - Skills: Financial analysis, investment management, corporate strategy
        
        3. Investment Banking:
           - BBA/B.Com → MBA → Analyst → Associate → VP → Director → MD
           - Entry: MBA from tier-1 institutes
           - Skills: Valuation, financial modeling, negotiation
