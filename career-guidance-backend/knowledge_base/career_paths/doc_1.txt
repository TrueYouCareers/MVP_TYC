
        Medical Career Paths in India:
        
        1. Clinical Practice:
           - MBBS → House Surgeon → MD/MS → Senior Resident → Consultant → Private Practice
           - Entry: MBBS through NEET-UG, PG through NEET-PG
           - Skills: Clinical skills, patient management, specialization expertise
        
        2. Research Path:
           - MBBS → MD/MS → Research Fellow → Scientist → Director
           - Entry: MBBS, MD/MS, PhD
           - Skills: Research methodology, scientific writing, specialization
        
        3. Public Health:
           - MBBS → MPH → Program Officer → Program Manager → Health Administrator
           - Entry: MBBS, MPH
           - Skills: Epidemiology, health policy, program management
