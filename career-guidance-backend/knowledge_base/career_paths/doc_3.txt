
        Arts & Humanities Career Paths in India:
        
        1. Law:
           - 5-year integrated law degree or 3-year LLB after graduation → Junior Associate → Associate → Partner
           - Entry: CLAT for 5-year program
           - Skills: Legal research, drafting, argumentation
        
        2. Media & Journalism:
           - BA (Journalism) → Reporter → Senior Reporter → Editor → Chief Editor
           - Entry: Bachelor's in Mass Communication/Journalism
           - Skills: Writing, research, media production
        
        3. Civil Services:
           - Any Bachelor's degree → UPSC preparation → Civil Services Exam → Probation → Career in IAS/IPS/IFS/etc.
           - Entry: UPSC Civil Services Examination
           - Skills: Administration, policy implementation, leadership
