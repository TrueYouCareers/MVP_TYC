
        Top Medical Colleges in India:
        
        1. AIIMS (All India Institute of Medical Sciences):
           - AIIMS Delhi, AIIMS Jodhpur, AIIMS Bhubaneswar, etc.
           - Entry through NEET-UG
           - Known for advanced medical research and education
        
        2. Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore:
           - One of the oldest and most prestigious
           - Entry through NEET-UG and college-specific test
        
        3. JIPMER (Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education & Research):
           - Puducherry campus
           - Entry through NEET-UG
        
        4. Maulana Azad Medical College, Delhi
        
        5. Lady Hardinge Medical College, Delhi
        
        6. Government Medical Colleges in Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai
