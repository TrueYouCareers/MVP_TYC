
        Top Engineering Colleges in India:
        
        1. IITs (Indian Institutes of Technology):
           - IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Madras, IIT Kanpur, IIT Kharagpur
           - Entry through JEE Advanced
           - B.Tech, M.Tech, and research programs
           - Average placements: ₹15-30 LPA
        
        2. NITs (National Institutes of Technology):
           - NIT Trichy, NIT Warangal, NIT Surathkal, NIT Calicut
           - Entry through JEE Main
           - B.Tech and M.Tech programs
           - Average placements: ₹8-20 LPA
        
        3. BITS (Birla Institute of Technology and Science):
           - Campuses in Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad
           - Entry through BITSAT
           - Known for flexible curriculum
           - Average placements: ₹10-25 LPA
        
        4. Other Top Engineering Colleges:
           - IIIT Hyderabad, Delhi Technological University, VIT Vellore, Jadavpur University
